🗼 Air Traffic Control Briefing

The Tower doesn't fly the plane — it controls the airfield. This view aggregates every AI model GitHub Copilot can dispatch: their strengths, limitations, surface availability, plan access and how the Copilot engine routes between them. Use it to pick the right model for the job instead of reaching for the default.
