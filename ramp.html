🛬 Ground Operations Briefing

The Ramp is where aircraft get serviced before flight — baggage, fueling, catering, pushback. In Copilot terms: autonomous agents, MCP tool execution, and the infrastructure that works without the pilot's hands on the controls. These instruments run in the background, on GitHub's infrastructure, or via external tool servers.
